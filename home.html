About the Project

This platform integrates real-time traffic and pollution monitoring data with smart routing algorithms to tackle urban challenges like congestion and air quality. By leveraging IoT technology and advanced data analytics, it dynamically optimizes traffic flow and reduces emissions, contributing to a sustainable urban environment.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Monitoring: Stay updated with live pollution levels and traffic conditions across the city.
  • Smart Routing: Discover the most efficient and eco-friendly routes based on real-time data.
  • Data Analytics: View detailed reports and trends on traffic and air quality.
  • Community Engagement: Learn how small changes in behavior can significantly impact urban sustainability.
